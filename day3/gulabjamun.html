Galub jamun

Ingredients:

  1. milk
  2. maida
  3. sugar
  4. ghee
  5. cardmom
  6. rosewater
  7. oil

Preparation:

  • Mix milk and maida to make a dough
  • Make small balls from the dough
  • heat oil in a pan and fry the balls until golden brown
  • Boil water and sugar to make sugar syrup
  • Add cardmom and rosewater to the syrup
  • Add balls to the syrup

Gulab jamun is a popular Indian dessert that is often enjoyed during festivals,

celebrations, and special occasions. Gulab jamun is known for its rich taste

and melt-in-the-mouth texture, making it a beloved dessert in Indian cuisine.
